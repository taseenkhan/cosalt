FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

Portable Services

  • How do I choose the right Free Zone for my business In the UAE?

    Choose based on industry, cost, location, and business needs. Some Free Zones specialize in tech, media, or logistics. Consulting an expert helps you select the best fit for your goals.

  • What are the costs involved in setting up a Free Zone company In the UAE?

    Costs include license fees, office space, visa processing, and registration. Prices vary by Free Zone and business type. Some offer budget-friendly packages, so comparing options is essential.

  • Do I need a local sponsor for my Free Zone business In Dubai?

    No, Free Zone businesses allow 100% foreign ownership without a local sponsor. However, selling in the UAE mainland requires a local distributor or a dual license.

  • Can I trade outside the UAE with a Free Zone license?

    Yes, Free Zone companies can trade internationally. However, selling directly in the UAE mainland requires a local distributor or an additional license from the authorities.
